faeshe

Musicians using faeshe get gigs through four channels:


▼Immediate hire

When a venue posts a date, a blast goes out to the qualifying acts. Acts qualify by genre, star rating, price, size. Respond to the blast and close the deal immediately.

▼Creating an EVENT

Venues post an open stage, and you compete with other acts by securing the minimum audience for the event. The first act to secure its audience wins the stage.

▼Posting availability in the STREAM

You can post your act in the stream for immediate hire. Venues searching for immediate dates will pick you up.

▼Having a profile resident in our searchable catalog

Venues and other patrons can search according to genre, star-rating, price, etc. They will see the profile you create, visit your portfolio, and hire from there.
